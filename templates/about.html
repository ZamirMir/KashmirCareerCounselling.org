The Group History

Kashmir's First Online Career,Counseling & Admission Services.

Our Company

Kashmir Career And Counseling Pvt Ltd.Popularly known as KCC, Kashmir's Educational Services started as a small entrepreneurial venture in 2018 with the vision of spreading educational awareness and providing admission guidance to students based on aptitude, Interest and personality. Our Signature services include Scientific Career Counselling, Admission Guidance, and Institutional Promotion.
We are a pioneer of Online Career and Counseling, first of its kind ever used in Kashmir for Career Counselling of students, based on which most suitable career path are advised to students.

Our Vision

Kashmir Career and Counseling offers unbiased, comprehensive, genuine, ethical and quality professional education services. We help connect students with institutions of higher learning through out India. we attract the best talent in terms of counselors and facilitators without distinction of race, religion or social status and offer a great working environment. We improve the lives of students while preparing them to be globally successful citizens.

Our Mission

To create an environment of Moral, Social, Cultural and Educational progress for realization of an Egalitarian Society.

Message

From Founders Desk

Dear Friends,
This age is moving at a breath taking Speed with Change in every Sphere of Human Life and education sector is not left apart. Today, there are lot more courses available for students apart from the conventional ones. With wide range of courses available and companies seeking specialized people for job choosing a career has become a serious and tedious affair. While selecting a career option it is important for student to discover their potential, aptitude and interest and select the right course accordingly which unfortunately, not many students are able to do so.
Keeping same difficulty in mind K.C.C has introduced one Stop Education Solution for. Using our platform a student can get career counselling and select career path most suited to them scientifically. Our counselling engages testing of three factors aptitude, interest and personality. Apart from this students can also access information about all courses and leading institutes offering them. We have added admission guidance services as well through which they can seek admission related information and also apply to these courses. Our entire model is made as such that Students who are unable to physically reach the institutes can yet overcome all the barriers and take an informed decision in clear, systematic, organized and authentic manner.
I Hope this Single Window Information Hub will bring a new era in Education Consultancy Industry.
